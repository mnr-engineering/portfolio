About Me

Hi, I'm Mo.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student who learns best by building real systems, breaking things, and figuring out how they work. My focus is hands-on engineering — turning theory into working hardware.

I’ve worked on projects involving aerodynamics, electronics integration, control systems, and mechanical design. From building a desktop wind tunnel to configuring a Cube Orange flight controller for a VTOL aircraft, I enjoy solving practical engineering problems.

My goal is to become an engineer who understands the full system — mechanical, electrical, and control — not just one piece of it.

What I Work With

  • Mechanical Design & Prototyping
  • Aerodynamics Fundamentals
  • Flight Controller Setup (Cube Orange / PX4 / ArduPilot)
  • Electronics Integration
  • CAD Modelling
  • Systems Thinking

How I Approach Engineering

I believe engineering is learned through building, testing, failing, and improving. Every project I take on is an opportunity to understand systems deeper — not just to make something that works, but to understand why it works.
